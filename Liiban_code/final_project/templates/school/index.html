School Finder aims to aid parents/guardians with the informed decision when selecting the right secondary school for their children we know this can be difficuly, below are some features that can help you.

intuitive Map

School-finder's intuitive map shows markers on all schools in England, each marker colour corresponds to the Ofsted rating, when you click on a marker you can learn more about the school on the side panel. If you know what type of school you're looking for please check out the maps filters.

League tables

All of our data is sourced directly from the Goverment education board, with the most recent grades acheived by students at each school, the league table allows for direct search for specific school. You can also filter the league table according to highest or lowest awarded schools by clicking on a column.

Ofsted grading

All schools in our database have an Ofsted rating, this is a third-party examining system, that involves school inspections by professionals, after inspections are carried out the school is awarded either: Outstanding, Good,requires improvement or Serious weakness, these are visible on the map.

About our Data

All data is sourced directly from the goverments education board, ensuring accurate and up to date data. we also use third-party school examinations such as Ofsted which grades schools such 'Outstanding' or 'requires improvement'. we also advise that you save schools that you're interested in so you don't have to search for it again.
